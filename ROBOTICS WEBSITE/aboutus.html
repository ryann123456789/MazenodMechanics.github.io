About our team

We are a dedicated team of high school students driven by a shared passion for STEM. Organized into specialized sub-teams, we collaborate seamlessly with guidance from our industry-experienced mentors. Our mission is to excel in the global FIRST Robotics Competition, a prestigious annual event. Our commitment to innovation, teamwork, and continuous learning fuels our journey as we design, build, and program robots to tackle complex challenges.

Sub Sections

Mechanical Team

The mechanical team is responsible for building the robot’s physical structure, ensuring precision in every component. This team offers students the chance to:

  • Design and create engineering plans
  • Test designs through both theoretical and physical methods
  • Build the robot
  • Maintain the robot throughout the season

The team is currently using a CAD software called OnShape solve problems and design solutions.

Programming Team

The programming team writes the code that controls the robot’s movements and systems, ensuring optimal performance. This team is in charge of:

  • Vision: Using cameras to gather valuable information for the robot.
  • Autonomous: Programming the robot to perform predetermined tasks.
  • Dashboard: Supplying the robot operators with essential data about the robot's status.

The team is currently coding in C++.

Media Team

The Media and Business Team is dedicated to highlighting the team's accomplishments, promoting our brand, and ensuring sponsors get the best value. This team works on:

  • Managing sponsor and partner relations
  • Photography
  • Video production and editing
  • Graphic design
  • Animation
  • Website maintenance and updates
